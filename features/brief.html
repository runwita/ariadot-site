The Brief

One short note, before the day gets loud.

Around 6am, a few calm lines land on your lock screen: what's worth a beat today, what came in overnight, what's coming this week. Read it over your first coffee, then get on with the day. There's an evening note too, to close the day off.

Written in your voice

Not a list of bullet points. A note that reads like a calm friend who went through your inbox so you didn't have to.

Only the near

Today and the rest of the week. The renewal eight months out stays quiet in the background until it's close.

Nothing to say, nothing sent

A quiet day gets a quiet inbox. Ariadot only writes when there's something genuinely worth your attention.
